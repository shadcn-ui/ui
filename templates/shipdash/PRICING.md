# ShipDash Pricing Strategy

## Price Point

**$49** (one-time payment)

### Why $49?

1. **Sweet spot for impulse buys** - Low enough that developers don't need approval
2. **Signals quality** - Not too cheap to seem like junk
3. **Compares favorably** - Similar products sell for $79-149
4. **Easy math** - If it saves you 4+ hours, it's worth it ($12.50/hr)

## License Terms

### Single License ($49)
- Unlimited personal projects
- Unlimited commercial projects
- Lifetime access to purchased version
- Use in client work
- No recurring fees

### What's NOT Allowed
- Reselling as a template/starter kit
- Sharing with team members (each dev needs a license)
- Creating a competing template product

## Upsell Opportunities

### 1. Team License ($149)
- Same as single license
- Up to 5 developers
- Priority support

### 2. Bundle (Future)
- ShipDash + other dashboard variants
- E-commerce dashboard
- Analytics dashboard
- Admin panel
- Bundle price: $99 (vs $147 individual)

### 3. Extended License ($199)
- Use in unlimited client projects
- White-label rights
- Remove attribution requirements

## Launch Pricing Strategy

### Week 1: Launch Special
- Price: **$29** (40% off)
- Create urgency
- Build initial reviews/social proof

### Week 2-4: Early Bird
- Price: **$39** (20% off)
- Transition period

### Month 2+: Regular Price
- Price: **$49**
- Occasional sales (Black Friday, etc.)

## Revenue Projections (Conservative)

| Scenario | Sales/Month | Revenue |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Low | 10 | $490 |
| Medium | 30 | $1,470 |

## Competition Analysis

| Product | Price | What They Offer |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Tailwind UI | $299 | Component library (no starter) |
| Shipfast | $199 | Full SaaS boilerplate (complex) |
| Indie starter kits | $79-149 | Similar to ShipDash |

**ShipDash positioning:** More affordable than complex boilerplates, more complete than component libraries.

## Payment Platform

**Gumroad** (Recommended)
- 10% + $0.50 per sale
- Built-in audience
- Easy setup
- Handles taxes

Alternative: Lemon Squeezy (8.9% + $0.50, better for EU)

## Refund Policy

**No refunds** - Digital product, code is delivered immediately.

Clear in product description:
> "Due to the digital nature of this product, all sales are final. Please review the demo and feature list before purchasing."
