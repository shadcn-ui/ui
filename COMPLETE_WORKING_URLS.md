## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

### Correct URL Patterns

‚ùå **WRONG:** `http://localhost:4000/erp/crm/leads`  
‚úÖ **CORRECT:** `http://localhost:4000/erp/sales/leads`

The CRM functionality is under the **Sales** section, not a separate CRM section!

### URL Structure
```
http://localhost:4000/erp/[module]/[feature]

Modules:
- sales (includes CRM: leads, opportunities, customers)
- product (inventory, catalog, suppliers)
- operations (manufacturing, supply chain, quality)
- hris (employees, payroll, leave)
- accounting (finances, budgets, reports)
- analytics (dashboards, reports)
- pos (point of sale)
- settings (configuration)
```
